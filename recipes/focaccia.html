Focaccia

Description

An italian flatbread made with olive oil and a variety of toppings

Ingredients

  • Tipo 00 flour
  • EVO oil
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Mix flour and salt
  2. Mix water and yeast
  3. Mix everything together and knead for 8 minutes
  4. Place into a bowl with EVO oil and fold
  5. Do 3 more folds 30 minutes apart
  6. Fold onto tray and dimple with more oil
  7. Leave to rise before baking for 20 minutes
